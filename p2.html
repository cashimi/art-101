Project 2: Timelapse Algomations

Phase 1: Algorithm Choices with Group

Our group explored several algorithmic approaches to help define the direction of our creative project. We focused on experimenting with 2 to 4 different algorithms, including flocking, forces, and brownian motion. Each algorithm offered unique visual and interactive possibilities. Through testing and discussion, we found ourselves most drawn to flocking due to their potential to align with our artistic goals and the themes we are interested in exploring. To support our exploration, we generated a set of sample images that demonstrate how we’ve been working with each algorithm. The images capture the early phase of our research, and help us clarify the tools and techniques we’re most likely to develop further.
